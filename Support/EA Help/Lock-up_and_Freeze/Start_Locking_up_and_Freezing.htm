Lock-up and Freeze Troubleshooting

Lockups and freezing can usually be treated as crashes but they are more likely to be related to file conflicts or hardware setup than crashes. There are different lockups that point to more specific issues and troubleshooting steps. The following section of the help troubleshooting manual will guide you through common solutions for Lockups and Freezing.
