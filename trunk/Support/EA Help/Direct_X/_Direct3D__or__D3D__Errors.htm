'Direct3D' or 'D3D' Errors

Direct3D Acceleration is a DirectX video card option. Errors such as these result from a conflict with your video card. To resolve these errors, follow these steps:

 

  • Open the DirectX Diagnostic Tool

Click here for steps on opening the DirectX Diagnostic Tool

  • Verify that you meet all requirements for the game, especially the video card requirements as this error relates to a video option

Click here for assistance locating the minimum requirements

  • On the Display tab, make sure that Direct3D Acceleration is set to Enabled.

    • Click the Enable button if it is set to Disabled

    • If Direct3D is N/A then you will need to contact your system or video card manufacturer to correct this issue.

  • Make sure you have the latest manufacturer approved video card drivers
