Locking up with a Repeating Sound

If the game locks up with a sound loop, it is likely a conflict with the sound card. Often the sound card's hardware acceleration must be adjusted to communicate properly with the game.

 

  • In this case it will be necessary to lower the system's sound hardware acceleration to Basic

Click here for steps on adjusting sound hardware acceleration

  • Additionally, it is important to ensure that all background tasks have been ended. Applications running in the background can result in random lockups occurring within the game.

Click here for steps on ending background tasks

  • After all background tasks have been ended it is advisable to empty the Windows Temp folder.

Click here for steps on emptying your Temp folder
