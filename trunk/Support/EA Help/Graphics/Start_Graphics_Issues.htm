Graphics Troubleshooting

Graphical corruption is usually a conflict with the video card or other display settings in the system. Graphical corruption is the outcome of a miscommunication between hardware and software, or the attempt to produce the needed graphics without the proper hardware and software support.

 

  • Verify that your video card meets all system requirements and it is using the newest manufacturer approved video drivers.

Click here for steps on updating drivers

  • Adjust your desktop resolution to 1024x768 for the best compatibility with your game

Click here for steps on changing your desktop resolution
